# Summary

`Training Schedule CRD` is a selective ingest of the canonical training schedules and generic checklist templates across PM, estimating, and sales tracks. It excludes personalized weekly checklist copies and recovered variants, focusing instead on the repeatable structure of the training program.

## Key Takeaways

- The PM and estimating schedule templates share the same sampled early-week structure, including organizational health, glossary and paint basics intro, safety travel, ETC/Epicor intro, and PM shadowing.
- The sales schedule diverges by week three into Salesforce, Zoom, marketing, and customer-engagement work.
- The NFO PM schedule makes the PM track explicit: org health, safety travel, finance immersion, estimating shadowing, PM shadowing, field training, Paint Basics quizzes, PM 101, PTO, and a formal PM 101 test.
- The repeated weekly checklist template functions as a lightweight planning artifact rather than a role-specific curriculum document.

## Open Questions

- The PM and estimating blank schedules may simply reuse a shared base template; deeper role-specific divergence may live in later weeks or other files.
- Personalized weekly checklist copies, autorecovered spreadsheets, and duplicate schedule variants were intentionally deferred from ingest.
