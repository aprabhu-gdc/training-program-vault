# Summary

`Helpful Travel Info` documents the mechanics and expectations for arranging apartments and hotels for crews. It translates Graydaze's travel-heavy operating model into a repeatable request process centered on the Graydaze App and timely, complete information.

## Key Takeaways

- Housing requests should be submitted as soon as needs are known.
- Requests require foreman identity, job number, dates, room configuration, jobsite address, and budget details.
- Foremen are expected to have their own rooms while crew members share rooms.
- Hotel incidentals require care, and the document explicitly warns against using the company card in the wrong way.
