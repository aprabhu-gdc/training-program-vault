# Summary

`Paint Basics Training` is a foundational coatings manual organized around paint basics, performance characteristics, and paint systems. It gives new PMs the vocabulary and supplier-facing product literacy needed to understand primers, finishes, data pages, and how coating systems are specified and used.

## Key Takeaways

- The manual is positioned as a starting point, not a complete technical education.
- It expects PMs to continue learning from data sheets, supplier resources, trade shows, and jobsite experience.
- The source treats technical product literacy as a PM success requirement rather than a specialist-only concern.
