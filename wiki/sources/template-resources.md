# Summary

`Template Resources` captures the reusable artifact layer behind Graydaze PM and mission-support workflows. These files are not narrative training documents; they are the calculators, forms, and precon checklists that operationalize change orders, travel, pre-mobilization review, billing, and extra-work authorization.

## Key Takeaways

- The CO and travel calculators make workers-comp, labor, travel, and gross-profit math explicit in a reusable format.
- `Graydaze Precon_` encodes a preconstruction review checklist around contract, schedule, submittals, testing, and access issues.
- `Ticket.pdf` is an extra-work authorization artifact for scope beyond the original contract.
- The folder shows that Graydaze relies on repeatable spreadsheets and forms to stabilize PM administration, not just verbal process knowledge.
