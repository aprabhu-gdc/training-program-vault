# Summary

`Job Site Glossary` is a practical vocabulary sheet for field terms, tools, materials, joints, and building conditions. It functions as a translation layer for new PMs who need to interpret jobsite language quickly.

## Key Takeaways

- The glossary covers equipment, sealant and slab terminology, dock and wall conditions, and common abbreviations.
- The sheet is designed as operational vocabulary support for training rather than as a formal specification source.
- Early distribution of the glossary is embedded into the onboarding and schedule materials.
