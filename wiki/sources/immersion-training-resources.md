# Summary

`Immersion Training Resources` covers the mission-support side of PM work: billing, underbilling and earned revenue, accounts payable, GC Pay change-order workflows, and lien-waiver and CCIP process expectations. It is practical finance and administration training for active project managers.

## Key Takeaways

- Billing cadence, retainage, pay-app formatting, and customer-specific form requirements are treated as core PM responsibilities.
- Underbilling and earned revenue are explained as project-finance outcomes that depend on sound ETC and billing discipline.
- GC Pay change-order creation has explicit rules for singular versus multi-line SOV changes and requires coordination with controller review.
- Lien waivers and CCIP are recurring compliance responsibilities tied to payment and risk management.
