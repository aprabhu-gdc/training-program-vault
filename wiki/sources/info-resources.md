# Summary

`Info Resources` is a mixed but high-signal operational folder. It contains practical PM guidance on schedule building, job startup, GC Pay process, customer and foreman communication, day-to-day organization, common foreman misses, PTO-aligned project flow notes, material sequencing rules, and repaint takeoff tips.

## Key Takeaways

- Several files sharpen the PM operating model already described in PM 101 by making planning and startup behaviors more concrete.
- `GC Pay Workshop Outline Update` gives a fuller workshop-style overview of project assignment, SOV creation, and pay-app handling in GC Pay.
- `How to build a schedule` and `How to successfully start and run a job` add practical sequencing and communication guidance that complements the formal checklists.
- `Peter Power to Outperform Notes` bridges PTO language to specific handoff, dump, SSSP, GC Pay, and pre-mob actions.
- `Organization and Time Management Workshop` shows that individual work habits are treated as an operational risk and trainable skill.
