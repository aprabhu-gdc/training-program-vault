# Summary

`Customer 101 CRD` is a first-week onboarding checklist focused on account setup, internal contacts, travel profiles, region context, and previewing the next training week. It behaves like a concrete translation of the broader onboarding schedules into actionable setup tasks.

## Key Takeaways

- Week one emphasizes logins, device and workspace setup, key corporate tools, business travel profiles, and receiving foundational materials like the glossary and paint basics.
- The checklist ties onboarding to travel-with-safety activity in the following week.
- The source shows how administrative setup is treated as part of customer readiness and not as separate HR overhead.
