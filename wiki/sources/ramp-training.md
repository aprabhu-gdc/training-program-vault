# Summary

`Ramp Training` introduces Graydaze's company-card philosophy, account activation flow, and operating guidelines. It combines values language with practical steps for registration, wallet setup, coding, notification settings, and support.

## Key Takeaways

- Ramp spending is framed as a values and accountability issue, not just a card-administration task.
- Users are expected to code purchases promptly, retain receipts, and ask questions when uncertain.
- The deck provides the activation path, support channels, and reminder to configure notifications and travel integrations.
