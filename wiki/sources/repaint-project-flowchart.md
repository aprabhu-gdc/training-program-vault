# Summary

`Repaint Project Flowchart` combines two process documents: one for the CRD sales process and one for dump-profit handling in new construction. Together they show how opportunities move from client contact through takeoff, estimating, handoff, initial ETC, and bonus-linked dump profit reporting.

## Key Takeaways

- Sales owns the client relationship and triages takeoffs, site presence, and handoff timing.
- Estimators prepare proposals, while sales follows up and updates Salesforce.
- Every job is expected to have a dump meeting before start.
- Initial ETC and dump profit are operationally important enough to feed a dedicated email flow and mission-support reporting cadence.
- Dump profit is tied to accountability and incentive structures for estimating and PM roles.
