# Summary

`Pre-Job Check Lists` is a high-signal operational folder that turns Graydaze process expectations into concrete startup, pre-dump, and scope-specific readiness lists. It covers both general PM lifecycle work and technical preparation for common paint, sealant, and joint-filler scopes.

## Key Takeaways

- The pre-dump and job-start documents make dump meetings, ETC agreement, SSSP preparation, travel setup, vendor coordination, and day-one field presence explicit expectations.
- The phase 1 and phase 2 process maps mirror the larger PM lifecycle described in PM 101.
- The technical checklists identify the tools, consumables, safety items, and job-specific equipment required for textures, apron joints, vertical joints, and joint filler work.
- The 10:1 startup sheet is a procedural aid for safe and correct texture-rig startup.
