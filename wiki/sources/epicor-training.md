# Summary

`Epicor Training` explains how Graydaze PMs access Epicor ECM and how they should review, approve, or deny invoices. It is a practical workflow document for day-to-day cost control rather than a general systems overview.

## Key Takeaways

- PMs can access Epicor via app or laptop with their Graydaze login.
- Invoice approval discipline matters: denials require reasons and should trigger vendor communication with AP copied when pricing, quantity, job coding, or PM assignment is wrong.
- Epicor is treated as part of efficient cost tracking, not separate from project management.
