# Summary

The `Graydaze PM Roles and Responsibilities` documents are a direct role checklist for PM ownership from job assignment through orders, crew setup, and day-one startup. They are more tactical than PM 101 and read like a role-memory aid for active job management.

## Key Takeaways

- PM ownership begins as soon as the job is assigned.
- The role centers on reviewing contract and scope, preparing SSSP and posted panel, scheduling and leading the dump meeting, submitting the initial ETC, and reconciling the SOV with the contract.
- PMs are expected to coordinate vendors, stores, lifts, caulking material, crew housing, and GC Pay setup.
- The checklist explicitly requires a pre-job site visit before the crew arrives and direct day-one involvement with the foreman and customer.
