# Current Synthesis

Field execution basics in this corpus cover the technical floor a PM is expected to stand on: paint-system vocabulary, sealant and joint-filler understanding, equipment familiarity, basic jobsite terminology, scope-specific readiness lists, and environmental constraints like winter thresholds.

## Main Components

- Paint basics and system vocabulary.
- Sealant categories, use cases, and failure-prevention guidance.
- Core equipment knowledge for texture, paint, pressure washing, crack chasing, and joint filler work.
- Scope-specific startup checklists for aprons, texture, vertical joints, joint filler, and general startup.
- Material sequencing windows, repaint takeoff observations, and recurring foreman misses that affect execution quality.
