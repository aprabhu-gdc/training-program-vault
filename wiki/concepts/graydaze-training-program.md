# Current Synthesis

The Graydaze training program is a staged operational apprenticeship rather than a simple reading curriculum. The PM track begins with organizational-health onboarding, account setup, vocabulary and paint basics, then moves through safety travel, finance and estimating immersion, PM shadowing, field training, PM 101 study, PTO completion, and testing.

## Structure

- Shared early onboarding covers office setup, glossary, paint basics, safety exposure, and core systems.
- The PM path combines mentor-led office training with repeated field immersion.
- Estimating and sales schedules share onboarding scaffolding but diverge into role-specific work.
- Weekly checklists and shadowing plans reinforce the training program as a managed cadence, not a one-time orientation.
