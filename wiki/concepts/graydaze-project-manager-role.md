# Current Synthesis

The Graydaze PM owns the project from handoff through execution and payment. The role blends contract and scope review, pre-mobilization planning, ETC ownership, customer and subcontractor communication, travel logistics, field presence, paperwork discipline, and day-one jobsite leadership.

## Core Responsibilities

- Prepare the job before mobilization: review contract, drawings, scope, finish schedule, safety plan, and projected dates.
- Lead the dump and establish the initial ETC.
- Build realistic schedules, two-week look-aheads, and pre-mob communication with the superintendent and foreman.
- Coordinate vendors, housing, GC Pay setup, equipment, materials, and foreman handoff.
- Be on site at key moments, especially day one and high-risk or high-visibility phases.
- Keep the office, subs, and clients informed while maintaining documentation, schedule updates, invoice review, and billing-related follow-through.
- Use standard forms, calculators, and precon artifacts to turn expectations into repeatable project controls.
