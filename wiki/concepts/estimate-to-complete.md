# Current Synthesis

Estimate to Complete (ETC) is the central Graydaze projection and accountability tool for project financial performance. It combines revised contract value, revised cost budget, costs to date, variance, and percent complete so PMs can forecast where a job will finish rather than merely report what has already happened.

## Practical Use

- Start from QuickBooks cost-to-date numbers and reconcile them to the ETC.
- Adjust revenue and costs for confident change-order expectations and known remaining work.
- Use variance and percent complete to identify busted budgets, missing costs, and optimistic assumptions.
- Treat ETC as both a management tool and a communication tool for reviews and accountability.

## Tensions

- The 2022 ETC material says updates are required twice per month minimum.
- The 2024 ETC deck says updates are required once a week minimum.
- Inference: the weekly 2024 cadence is the newer standard, but the older cadence should remain documented as a prior practice.
