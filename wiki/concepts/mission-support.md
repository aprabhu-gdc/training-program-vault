# Current Synthesis

Mission Support is the administrative and financial operating layer that PMs are expected to understand and participate in directly. In this corpus it includes billing, underbilling and earned revenue, accounts payable, invoice review, ETC maintenance, GC Pay change orders, lien waivers, CCIP, and company-card discipline.

## Key Themes

- PM work includes getting the job paid, not just getting it built.
- Financial systems are interconnected: QuickBooks feeds ETC, Epicor supports invoice review, GC Pay drives CO and pay-app workflows, and Ramp supports card spending and coding.
- Administrative accuracy is treated as operational discipline rather than clerical afterthought.
- Templates, org charts, and standard forms provide the artifact layer that makes mission-support work repeatable.
