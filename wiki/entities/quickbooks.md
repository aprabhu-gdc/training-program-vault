# Current Synthesis

QuickBooks appears in this branch as the accounting source for costs to date, invoice history, and project creation. ETC work is repeatedly described as beginning with QuickBooks numbers and then reconciling them to the operational forecast.

## Main Uses

- provide cost-to-date inputs for ETC
- support invoice and anomaly review
- anchor job setup and project-level accounting data
- help verify pay-app and billing support documents
