# Current Synthesis

The CX, EX, and FX teams appear in this corpus as named support functions around customer back-office work, employee onboarding and records, travel coordination, pre-job audits, prequalifications, licensing, and related support work that surrounds PM execution.

## Main Roles

- CX: customer-facing back-office relationships, prequalifications, licensing, audits, and related support
- EX: onboarding, records, benefits, finance training coordination, and employee support
- FX: field travel arrangements and support logistics
