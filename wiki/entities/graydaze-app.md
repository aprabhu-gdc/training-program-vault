# Current Synthesis

The Graydaze App is the internal operational app referenced across onboarding, travel, safety, GC Pay support, project startup, and field paperwork. It functions as a workflow entry point rather than a single-purpose tool.

## Main Uses

- request housing and travel-related support
- complete field and safety forms
- support PM pre-task and job-start workflows
- initiate certain GC Pay and project-admin actions
