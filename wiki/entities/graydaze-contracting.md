# Current Synthesis

Graydaze Contracting appears in this corpus as a nationwide, travel-heavy contractor whose operating model is built around project-manager ownership, field subcontractor coordination, and a branded customer experience. The organization treats culture, financial discipline, travel logistics, and technical field knowledge as integrated parts of the same PM role.

## Characteristics

- PM-led operating model with strong ownership expectations.
- Heavy use of travel, shadowing, and field immersion in training.
- Internal systems and workflows across Graydaze App, GC Pay, Epicor, QuickBooks, Ramp, and Salesforce.
- Repeated emphasis on the `Graydaze Experience`, organizational health, teamwork, and relationship management.
- Named support teams and specialists play visible roles around startup, customer back-office work, onboarding, finance, and travel logistics.
