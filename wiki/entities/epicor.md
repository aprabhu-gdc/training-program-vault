# Current Synthesis

Epicor ECM is the invoice-review and cost-tracking system described in this branch. PMs use it to review vendor invoices, approve or deny them with reasons, and support disciplined cost control.

## Main Uses

- log in through app or browser with Graydaze credentials
- review vendor invoices against job, quantity, and pricing reality
- approve or deny invoices with explanatory notes
- coordinate invoice issues with accounts payable
