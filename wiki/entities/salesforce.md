# Current Synthesis

Salesforce appears here as the sales and opportunity-tracking system. It is tied to bid follow-up, dump-profit reporting, and the sales training track rather than to PM execution directly.

## Main Uses

- update opportunities and sales progress
- support sales process visibility
- receive dump-profit reporting inputs for certain new-construction workflows
- support sales-track onboarding and reporting habits
